Map Listings

The EPG123 Client Guide Tool gives you the capability to map any lineup channel (right side) to the WMC tuner channel (left side) giving you complete and easy control of your guide. Just select a lineup channel on the right, select the WMC guide channel(s) on the left, right-click and select 'Subscribe'. There is also a quick option to click the Match by: [# Number] button to automatically map all lineup channels shown on the right with all the tuner channels on the left by channel number.
